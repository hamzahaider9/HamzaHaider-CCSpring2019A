Welcome to Thanos vs Captain America 2-player game.
You can either play as Thanos by using keys W and S 
to move Thanos up and down.
Or you can play as Captain America by using the up 
and down arrow keys.

Your objective is to deflect Thanos's hand. The 
player who is able to prevent the opponent from 
deflecting Thanos's hand gets one point.

The player to get 3 points first wins.

Click here to start the game.
